Navigate China with Confidence

Unlocking the Middle Kingdom

China is a land of extraordinary contrasts, where ancient dynasties meet technological innovation. From the iconic Great Wall and the Terracotta Warriors of Xi'an to the futuristic skyline of Shanghai and the tech hubs of Shenzhen, China offers unparalleled opportunities for business and tourism. However, the Chinese visa application process requires careful preparation, particularly regarding the COVA (China Online Visa Application) form and the specific documentation requirements.

World Immigration's China visa experts are well-versed in the unique requirements of the Chinese embassy. We assist with both L (Tourist) and M (Business) visa categories, ensuring your COVA form is completed accurately, your photos meet the strict Chinese specifications (33mm x 48mm with white background), and your supporting documents, including invitation letters and business certifications, are properly formatted and authenticated.

Business Visa & Invitation Support

For business travellers, the M visa requires a formal invitation letter from a Chinese entity, which must contain specific details about the host company, purpose of visit, and duration of stay. Our Premium and Elite packages include comprehensive invitation letter review and, where needed, liaison with Chinese business partners to ensure the invitation meets all embassy requirements. We also assist with visa applications for trade fairs, exhibitions, and long-term multiple-entry business visas.

Our Elite package features an in-person escort to the Chinese Visa Application Service Centre (CVASC), priority appointment scheduling, and visa delivery to your doorstep upon approval. For groups and corporate delegations, we offer bulk processing services with a dedicated account manager. Our post-visa services include travel guidance on Chinese customs regulations, currency exchange, and recommended business etiquette. World Immigration is your comprehensive partner for all things China.
