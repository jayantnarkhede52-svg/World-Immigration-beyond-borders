Post-Study Work Visas

Top destinations like the UK (2 years), Canada (up to 3 years), and Australia offer generous post-study work rights, allowing you to gain international work experience.

Part-Time Work

Most countries allow international students to work up to 20 hours per week during the semester and full-time during holidays, helping manage living expenses.

Scholarships

Indian students are eligible for numerous country-specific and university-specific scholarships, significantly lowering the cost of education.

Global Career

Degrees from recognized international universities are highly valued, giving you a competitive edge in the global job market.
