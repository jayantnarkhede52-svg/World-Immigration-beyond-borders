Your Path to Australia

The Australian Immigration Landscape

Australia's immigration system is sophisticated, points-based, and designed to attract skilled professionals, students, and genuine visitors. Whether you're applying for a Subclass 600 Visitor visa for tourism or family visits, a Subclass 500 Student visa for education, or a Skilled Migration visa through the SkillSelect portal, each pathway has its own set of requirements and documentation standards.

At World Immigration, our Australia specialists have extensive experience with the Department of Home Affairs' ImmiAccount system. We guide you through the entire online application process, help you compile a comprehensive evidence portfolio, and ensure your health and character requirements are met. Our expertise extends to complex cases including applicants with prior visa history in other countries, self-employed professionals, and family-sponsored visitors.

Skilled Migration & Student Visa Experts

Australia's General Skilled Migration (GSM) program is one of the most popular pathways for Indian professionals seeking permanent residency. Our Elite package includes comprehensive skills assessment guidance through relevant Australian assessing authorities, IELTS/PTE preparation resources, and strategic Expression of Interest (EOI) management. We help you identify the right occupation on the Skilled Occupation List (SOL) and maximize your points for age, experience, and qualifications.

For student visa applicants, we provide specialized Genuine Temporary Entrant (GTE) statement preparation, Confirmation of Enrolment (CoE) verification, Overseas Student Health Cover (OSHC) arrangement, and financial capacity demonstration. Our post-visa services include pre-departure briefings, airport assistance, and settlement guidance to help you transition smoothly into Australian life.
