Experience the Magic of Japan

The Land of the Rising Sun

Japan is a captivating blend of ancient tradition and cutting-edge modernity. From the serene temples of Kyoto and the iconic Mount Fuji to the neon-lit streets of Shibuya and the world-class cuisine that ranges from Michelin-starred sushi to hearty ramen, Japan offers an experience unlike any other. For Indian travellers, the Japanese visa application process requires careful attention to detail, particularly regarding the day-wise itinerary and financial documentation.

World Immigration's Japan visa specialists are experts in preparing the specific documentation that Japanese embassies expect. We create detailed, day-by-day itineraries that cover your accommodation, transportation, and sightseeing plans. Our financial document preparation ensures your bank statements, income tax returns, and employment letters are presented in a format that aligns with Japanese consular expectations.

Precision Documentation for Success

Japanese embassies are known for their meticulous review of visa applications. Unlike many other countries, Japan requires a precise day-wise itinerary with confirmed hotel bookings and transportation details. Our Premium and Elite packages include professional itinerary creation that showcases a well-planned, culturally respectful journey. We also prepare you for common scenarios such as visiting friends, attending business events, or participating in cultural exchange programs.

Our Elite package includes exclusive travel perks such as JR Pass purchase guidance, personalized day-plan creation based on your interests, and VFS Express Service for faster processing. We also provide post-visa travel briefings covering Japanese customs, etiquette, transportation navigation, and recommended experiences. With World Immigration, your Japan visa is the beginning of a transformative cultural adventure.
