Discover the Land of Smiles

Thailand: A Paradise for Every Traveller

Thailand is one of the most popular tourist destinations in the world, drawing millions of visitors annually with its pristine beaches, ornate temples, legendary Thai cuisine, and vibrant nightlife. From the bustling streets of Bangkok and the ancient ruins of Ayutthaya to the crystal-clear waters of Phuket and Krabi, and the lush jungles of Chiang Mai, Thailand offers an incredibly diverse travel experience at exceptional value.

For Indian passport holders, Thailand offers both Visa on Arrival (VOA) and e-visa options. The VOA allows a 15-day stay and is available at select airports, while the e-visa (TR category) permits a 60-day stay and can be extended. World Immigration simplifies this process by handling the entire application, ensuring your photographs, financial proof, and travel documents meet the exact specifications required by Thai immigration authorities.

Beyond Tourism: Business & Long-Stay Visas

Thailand's growing economy and strategic location in ASEAN make it an attractive destination for business professionals and digital nomads alike. World Immigration assists with Non-Immigrant B (Business) visas, Non-Immigrant ED (Education) visas, and the increasingly popular Thailand Elite visa for long-term residents. Our team handles the complex documentation including invitation letters from Thai companies, work permit coordination, and BOI visa facilitation for investors.

Our Elite package includes complete arrival concierge services: VIP fast-track through Suvarnabhumi Airport immigration, private limousine transfer to your hotel, a complimentary Thai SIM card with data, and 24/7 WhatsApp support throughout your stay. Whether you're visiting for a week-long beach holiday or establishing a long-term business presence, World Immigration ensures your Thailand visa experience is smooth, fast, and worry-free.
