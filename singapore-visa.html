Your Gateway to Singapore

The Lion City Awaits

Singapore is one of the world's most cosmopolitan cities, renowned for its stunning skyline, world-class shopping, multicultural cuisine, and efficient public infrastructure. Whether you're visiting for business meetings at Marina Bay Financial Centre, exploring the futuristic Gardens by the Bay, savouring hawker centre delicacies, or attending a major conference at the Sands Expo, Singapore offers an experience that is both modern and culturally rich.

Indian passport holders require a visa to enter Singapore, which is processed through an authorised agent. World Immigration is a trusted facilitator for Singapore e-visas, ensuring your application is submitted correctly with all required documentation. Our team handles the V39A form (Letter of Introduction), manages the SG Arrival Card, and ensures your financial documents meet the Immigration & Checkpoints Authority requirements.

Business & Long-Term Stay Expertise

Beyond tourist visas, World Immigration offers specialized support for business travellers and professionals seeking long-term passes in Singapore. We assist with Employment Pass (EP) applications, S Pass filings, and Long-Term Visit Pass (LTVP) submissions. Our Elite package includes a dedicated case manager who navigates the complex MOM (Ministry of Manpower) requirements and ensures your application stands out.

For business visitors attending conferences or trade shows, our Premium package includes same-day processing, SG Arrival Card pre-registration, and WhatsApp-based real-time updates. Our concierge services extend to hotel and meeting room booking assistance, ensuring your Singapore trip is productive from the moment you land. With World Immigration's expertise, your Singapore visa is just the beginning of a seamless travel experience.
